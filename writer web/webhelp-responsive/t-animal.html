How to have an Animal Presentation in AirClassroom

These instructions will show you how to safely present with animals in an AirClassroom

  • 4 Cages
  • 2 Aquariums
  • 1 Python
  • 1 Hawk
  • 1 Turtle
  • 1 Baby Leopard
  • 1 Tucan
  • 6 Blankets
  • Animal Treats
  • Broom
  • Trash Bag
  • Temporary AirClassroom Key
  1. Put all animals in cages and cover them with blankets before presenting
    Put blankets over the animal's cages so they are not freightened by the projector lights as they are especially bright
  2. Tell your audience that animals are freightened easily by loud noises and that they need to be quiet during the presentation
  3. Proceed to present all materials involving the projector first
  4. Present each animal (python, hawk, turtle, baby leopard, and tucan) one by one
    After you take off one blanket on the animal you present, remember to put it back on its cage or aquarium when you move to the next one
  5. Keep animals distracted with treats so they do not get restless
  6. Let audience members ask questions then touch some of the animals
    Don't let audiences touch the hawk as it is much more dangerous than the other animals
  7. Finish your presentation
  8. Clean up any animal droppings with the broom and trash bag found in the closet
  9. Take animals outside of the classroom
  10. Lock the door with your temporary key
