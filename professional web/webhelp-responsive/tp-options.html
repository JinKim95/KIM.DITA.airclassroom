Rental Classroom Packages

There are three tier packages that you can purchase from AirClassroom, and they all come with different perks.

As for pricing, Virginia Tech realizes different consumers have different budgets, and we are willing to work for an affordable price. Call us for more rental information.

Phone (for rental information): 333-222-3321

Everyone will start at the Basic Tier package, but one can upgrade at the receptionists desk at anytime. Call us for more upgrade information and purchasable items information and for additional questions.

Receptionist (for purchase of additional items or package upgrades): 219-535-1234

Receptionist (for help): 334-342-3255

The first tier is the Basic Tier and this includes the most essential goods:
  • Classroom itself
  • 2 Tables
  • 50 Seats
  • 1 Laser Pointer
  • 1 Projector
  • 1 HDMI Cable
  • 1 Guest Virginia Tech ID
  • Cleaning Supplies
  • (Only given to professionals in their field) 1 Technical Helper Using Technical Helpers

The second tier is the Medium Tier and this includes extra goods:

  • Classroom itself
  • 1 Laser Pointer
  • 1 Projector
  • 1 HDMI Cable
  • 1 Guest Virginia Tech ID
  • 1 Rental Laptop
  • 1 Technical Helper (Helps setup your presentation, can assist with multiple tasks)
  • Assorted food and drinks for the audience
  • Cleaning Supplies

The third and final tier is the Luxury Tier and this includes luxuary goods:

  • Classroom itself
  • 1 Laser Pointer
  • 1 Projector
  • 1 HDMI Cable
  • 1 Guest Virginia Tech ID
  • 1 Rental Laptop
  • 2 Technical Helpers (Helps setup your presentation)
  • Catered food and drinks for the audience
  • Mini Bar (provided to 21 and over only)
  • Juice Bar (If not 21)
  • Gift Bags for the audience
  • Cleaning Supplies
Various items, more complex items can also be rented out at the receptionist for $10 per hour:
  • Professional Lighting Set (includes 2 lights, 1 reflector, 1 large backdrop also can be rented separately)
  • Clip-on Microphones
  • Professional Cameras
  • Disco Ball (includes tape to put up)
  • Party Lights (includes tape to put up)
  • Touch Screen Laptops
  • Sound System Set (includes 2 HD stereos and their stands, 2 audio jacks, 1 mircophone also can be rented separately)
