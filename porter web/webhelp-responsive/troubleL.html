Troubleshooting the Rental Laptop

The laptop is not working properly

The laptop is not plugged in

  1. Plug in the laptop power cord into the electrical wall socket
  2. Use laptop properly

The laptop's battery is dead

  1. Go back to the receptionist with your laptop and dead battery
  2. Tell the receptionist that the battery is dead
  3. Receive a replacement battery from the receptionist
  4. Put new battery into the back of the laptop
  5. Use laptop properly

The laptop has a virus

  1. Go back to the receptionist with your laptop
  2. Tell the receptionist that the laptop has a virus and doesn't work
  3. Receive a new rental laptop from the receptionist
  4. Use laptop properly
