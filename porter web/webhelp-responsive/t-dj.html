How to Set up a DJ Presentation

These instruction will show you how to set up a DJ presentation with perfect sound reverberation in one of the AirClassrooms

Rent the Sound System set or pieces of it from the receptionist if you do not have one or more of the items required
  • 2 HD Stereos and their Stands
  • 1 Table
  • 2 Audio Jacks
  • 1 Chalkboard (comes with classroom)
  • 1 Mircophone
  • 1 Disco Ball with Tape(Optional)
  • 1 Set of Party Lights with Tape (Optional)
  • 1 Turntable
  • 1 Turntable USB Cord (comes attached to turntable)
  • 1 Laptop
  1. Set up the stereos on their stands
  2. Put the stereos next to the table in the front of the room, one on each side
  3. Put your turntable onto the one of the table's provided
  4. Plug in your turntable to a socket
  5. Place your laptop on the table and turn it on

  7. Plug in the mircophone to the computer
  8. Plug the audio jacks into the stereos
  9. Plug the other end of the audio jack into the laptop
  10. (Optional) Hang up the disco ball with tape on the chalkboard behind you
  11. (Optional) Hang up the party lights near the disco on the top side of the chalkboard behind you
  12. Rave
