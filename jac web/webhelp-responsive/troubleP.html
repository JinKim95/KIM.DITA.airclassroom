Troubleshooting the Projector

The projector is not working properly

The projector is not plugged in

Plug in the projector's cord into the wall socket

The projector has overheated

  1. Turn off the projector by flipping off the power button on its right side
  2. Wait for the red on top of the projector indicating overheating turns off
  3. Turn on the projector by hitting the power button on the right side of the projector
  4. Resume using the projector as normal

The projector is not digitally connected to my computer (didn't wait for drivers to install before use)

  1. Turn off the projector by flipping off the power button on its right side
  2. Unplug the USB cord attached to the projector to the computer
  3. Turn on the projector by hitting the power button on the right side of the projector
  4. Wait for the projector to restart
  5. Plug in the USB cord from the project to the computer
  6. Wait for the drivers to install all the way
  7. Use projector
